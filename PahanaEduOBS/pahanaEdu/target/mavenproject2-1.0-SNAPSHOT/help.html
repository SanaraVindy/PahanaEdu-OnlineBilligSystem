Help & Support Center

Go to the login page, enter your username and password, and click "Login". Make sure your role is selected correctly.

Navigate to the "Customer Management" section and click "Add New Customer". Fill out the required details and save.

Go to the "Billing" section, select or search for a customer, add items with quantities, and click "Generate Bill".

Click on the "Reports" tab to view visual and tabular summaries. Filters and date ranges are available for detailed insights.
